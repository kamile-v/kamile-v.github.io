One problem that I found challenging to solve in System's Design was determining the feasibility for my proposed application PlannerBird.
As someone that has not worked on developing software before, I had a difficult time coming up with an assessment for the technical, resource,
schedule, organizational, and legal feasibility of the project.
Luckily, with careful attention to detail and a lot of learning, I was able to come up with an assessment. Whether it would be accurate in the
real world or not, I now have experience considering various possibilities that could affect the outcome of a software development project.
I hope to get more experience doing this in the future. If interested, the System Specification for Plannerbird is attached in the Projects
section on this website.
